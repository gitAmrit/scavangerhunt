About

The mission of Elevation for Success is to empower low income students in the Middle Peninsula and Northern Neck area to access higher education through guidance, tutoring, and college preparedness. To fulfill the mission of Elevation for Success, we will offer professional training, apprenticeship and preparation programs for students grades 9th-12th.

Programs Offered

Rising 9th Graders

Our main focus for 9th graders is to help them develop a plan for their future.

During the summer, rising 9th graders will be attending Camp Leap In-I. During Camp Leap In-I, rising 9th graders will be attending workshops that will help them become academically successful during high school.

Workshops will be held Monday-Thursday from 8am-12pm

Workshop topics include:

  • How to get into Early College during high school
  • What is SAT/ACT? How and why is it important
  • What classes you should be taking to get ahead
  • Why college or trade school is important to my future
  • Overview of what to expect in 9th grade

**Additional workshops will be added over time

During the school year, 9th graders will attend E.F.S two Saturdays a month. During Saturday sessions, 9th graders will be teamed up with their advisor(s) to make sure their school year is going smoothly. During our Saturday sessions, students will have the opportunity to receive additional tutoring and guidance for other school related topics.

Rising 10th Graders

Our main focus for 10th graders is to help them officially start their college journey.

During the summer, rising 10th graders will be attending Camp Leap In-II. During Camp Leap In-II, rising 10th graders will be attending workshops that will focus on their academic success.

Workshops will be held Monday-Thursday from 8am-12pm

Workshop topics includes:

  • How to plan for Early College classes or AP Classes
  • How to create a resume
  • Volunteer opportunities
  • Goal setting academically and socially

** Additional workshops will be added over time

During the school year, 10th graders will attend E.F.S. two Saturdays a month. During Saturday sessions, 10th graders will be teamed up with their advisor(s) to make sure their school year is going smoothly. During our Saturday sessions, students will have the opportunity to receive additional tutoring. They will also have sessions that will help them with the following:

  • Applying for Early College
  • Plan to register for AP classes

**Additional help can be provided case by case

** Additional workshops will be added over time

During the school year, 12th graders will attend E.F.S. two Saturdays a month. During Saturday sessions, 12th graders will be teamed up with their advisor(s) to make sure their school year is going smoothly. During our Saturday sessions, students will have the opportunity to receive additional tutoring.
