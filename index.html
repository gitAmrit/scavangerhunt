Head out on the Capital Trail parallel to Dock St. from Great Shiplock Park

Find a tin-roofed entrance to information about the Richmond Dock and Chapel Island on your left.

Find and collect a hidden ticket!

Bonus: A photo of a team member doing a yoga pose by the river (2 POINTS)

Bonus +1 points if you get a Riverfront Canal Cruise boat in the picture!

Get back on the trail and stop at the historical marker about the Libby Prison.

Find and collect a hidden ticket!

Bonus: How did some Union soldiers escape the prison during the Civil War? (2 POINTS)

Back on the trail, follow the trail behind the floodwall (DO NOT GO ON THE ROAD) – There should be a small memorial to your left and art sculptures to your right.

Find and collect a hidden ticket!

Bonus: Take a picture of a team member with all of the sculpture in the frame.

Continue on the trail – Go through the doors in the flood wall (GET OFF THE BIKE TO GO THROUGH IF YOU NEED TO – DON’T RUN INTO PEOPLE OR DOORS)

Take a left and go up the path (WATCH FOR PEDESTRIANS) then take a right and continue up the path

Stop at the historical marker titled: African Americans and the Waterfront

Find and collect a hidden ticket!

Bonus: Take a picture of the Main St. Station clock tower from this spot (2 POINTS)

Continue down the path and take the path to cross the bridge on your left! (BE CAREFUL WITH THOSE TURNS – Walk with your bike if there are too many pedestrians on the bridge)

Continue down the Canal Walk – Stop at the Use of Arms historical marker

Find and collect a hidden ticket!

Go down the Canal Walk, take the bridge to get to the other side of the Canal Walk, and take the path(or stairs) up – You should end up at George Washington’s Vision historical marker

Bonus: Origami a paper boat and record a video of a team member “floating” it down the river (2 POINTS)

Follow the red brick road to Virginia St.- Take a left – You should be in a parking lot.

Bonus: Take a picture with man’s best friend (hint: it’s a mural) (2 POINTS)

Head out of the parking lot towards the Canal Walk Murals

Bonus: Take a picture from the Pipeline Overlook (Right across the Trask Railroad mural) (2 POINTS)

Take the road towards the murals

Bonus: Group photo with as many murals as you can (2 POINTS per photo)

Continue through the murals and take the bridge towards Casa del Barco

Go past the Christopher Newport Cross towards the parking lot with the murals

Left on Virginia St.

Right on the little red brick road towards the George Washington’s Vision historical marker

End at the bike share to collect your FINAL TICKET
